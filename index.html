Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How can I locate a specific gravesite?

To locate a gravesite, use our advanced search feature on the map page. Enter the name or other details of the deceased, and our system will guide you to the exact location with turn-by-turn directions.

What services do you offer for grave maintenance?

We offer comprehensive grave care services, including:

  • Regular cleaning and debris removal
  • Seasonal flower placement and maintenance
  • Headstone cleaning and restoration
  • Custom maintenance packages

You can schedule these services through your account dashboard.

How do I get notifications for important dates?

Our system sends automatic reminders for significant dates such as death anniversaries and birthdays. Ensure your contact information is up-to-date in your account settings to receive these notifications via email, SMS, or push notifications.

What are your cemetery's opening hours?

Our cemetery is open from 5:00 AM to 12:00 AM daily, allowing you to visit and pay your respects at a time that is convenient for you.

Can I create memorial pages for my loved ones?

Yes, our platform allows you to create beautiful digital memorial pages where you can share photos, stories, and memories. These pages can be public or private, depending on your preferences.
